MINING OPERATIONS

Gold Mining: Based in Sierra Leone, we mine some of the highest-quality gold in West Africa using open-pit and underground methods. Our Golden Ridge Mine utilizes bulk mining techniques for a projected output of 150,000 ounces annually.

Diamond Recovery: Our Diamond Lake Operation uses kimberlite extraction to yield the world’s finest rough diamonds. We strictly adhere to the Kimberley Process to ensure conflict-free sourcing.
